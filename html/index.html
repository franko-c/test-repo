Behavioural Psychology in Customer Service

An introduction

Have you ever picked up an instrument, played a note, and found it to be a most rewarding sound?

Perhaps you have experienced the phenomenon known as 'beginners luck', where your first attempt at something has you believe that you can drop everything and become a world leader in that activity?

These moments highlight some of the themes explored in the realm of behavioural psychology, where observable behaviours, and the results of those actions, are explored to form a basis of ideas that help us better understand what pushes us to do certain things, react a certain way and even develop lifelong habits.
